Welcome!

Welcome to VictorianRailPhotos.com, here we host hundreds of photos of Locomotives and Carriages in the state of Victoria, Australia. Our archive includes photos for most Metro and V/Line trains, as well as a growing collection of freight and heritage locomotives. We also have a large collection of photos of railway stations across Victoria, including suburban and regional stations.

Use the search menu on the right to find photos, or browse through the list below to see all the trains we have photos of below.
